A) Introduction - top

SeoBin is a cFinance, Consulting & Business template specially designed for Finance, Accounting, Financial, Brokerage, Accountant, Consulting Firms, Insurance, Loan, Tax help, Investment firm etc. It also comes with twitter bootstrap 4, SASS, Gulp, Video Popup, Beautiful Tab, Gallery and more. BiziPress is a solid All-in-One HTML Template for better experience.

Template Features

  • 9 Home Variations
  • 9 Different Header Layout
  • Multipage Design
  • HTML5 Validated
  • Based on Twitter Bootstrap 4
  • Responsive Template
  • FontAwesomeicons
  • Google Web Fonts
  • Contact Form
  • Stylist News Page
  • Documentation
  • Easy to customize and user friendly

How to change Contact Form Email Address for own?

  1. Please, open contact-form.php
  2. Find line 4 you will see
    $recipient = "yourmail@domain.com";
  3. You should change 'yourmail@domain.com' with your own.
